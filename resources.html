Portals and Handbooks

  • CS&E TA Portal Home

    The main hub for all CS&E undergraduate TA resources, announcements, and internal documentation. Start here for a high-level overview of TA operations and links to everything you might need.

  • Official CS&E UG TA Handbook Official

    The authoritative handbook published by the CS&E department. Covers academic integrity, grading policies, professional conduct, and TA responsibilities in full. Worth reading at the start of each semester.

  • TA Handbook PDF

    A downloadable PDF version of the TA Handbook, useful for offline reference or if you want a local copy to annotate and keep on hand.

Onboarding and Training

  • TA Hiring and Onboarding Document

    Step-by-step onboarding instructions for new TAs, including hiring paperwork, system access setup for MyU, Canvas, and Gradescope, and a first-week checklist. If you are new, start here.

  • UG TA Training Notes

    Notes compiled from TA training sessions covering expectations for labs, office hours, grading standards, and common student questions. A good read for new TAs before their first week on the job.

Forms, Templates, and Scheduling

  • UG TA Forms and Templates

    A collection of official forms and document templates TAs may need throughout the semester, including grade dispute templates, late work forms, and accommodation-related documents.

  • TA Office Hours Spreadsheet

    The shared schedule showing all TA office hours across the department. Use this to reserve your own slot, coordinate with other TAs, avoid scheduling conflicts, and find coverage when needed.

Reporting and Compliance

  • Report Misconduct / EOAA Important

    The University's Equal Opportunity and Affirmative Action office. Use this to report academic dishonesty, harassment, discrimination, or other misconduct. As a TA you are a responsible employee, take some time to understand your reporting obligations before the semester starts.
